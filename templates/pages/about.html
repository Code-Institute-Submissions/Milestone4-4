About Us

Put very simply, we are a local run family and community orientate health & performance facility. We facilitate all things fitness and of course Olympic Weightlifting, hence PHP Barbell. So what makes us stand out above the rest?

We Care. We care about you, about our coaches, about our methods, about our community and about openness. We want to impart the knowledge you need to learn about the thing you are doing. With Olympic Weightlifting technique and maintaining impeccable form is paramount especially when the load gets heavy. Industry secrets help no-one. So we make it our jobs to not have any! Any client who puts their trust in us to help them can rest assured knowing they will be provided with all the support necessary to succeed. Now, let's load a barbell!

Olympic Weightlifting

Here at PHP Barbell we specialise in teaching Olympic Weightlifting. We all learn together, and even our coaching methods evolve with every passing person we train in PHP Barbell. Olympic Weightlifting consists of two primary movements ......yes two.

The Snatch, and Clean & Jerk. Both movements on paper seem relatively easy. They aren't. Luckily our resident coach Danny has a solid hold on all of us and is super eager to impart his well earned knowledge of the sport onto anyone willing to learn! We have multiple members all lifting at a competitive standard and even our coaches compete. We just love the sport, so much so we do it daily. Have a look at some of the images below to see what our members and coaches do and if you are interested, why not take that deep dive into the wonderful world of Olympic Weightlifting at PHP Barbell.
